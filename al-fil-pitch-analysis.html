What to listen for

Tonal Center

Note the lowest sustained pitch each reciter settles on — this is their tonic. The pitch chart y-axis shows Hz; compare where each reciter's line clusters at the start and end of each ayah.

Maqam Signature

Al-Minshawi (Mujawwad) typically uses Bayati or Hijaz — listen for the characteristic raised 4th and lowered 3rd. Abdul Basit is known for Bayati with Hijaz modulations.

Cadential Patterns

Watch the pitch drop at the end of each ayah line. How steep is the descent? Does it land on the tonic, or on a higher finalis? This varies significantly across recitation schools.

Ornamentation

Rapid pitch oscillations in the chart (jagged sections) indicate melisma or tahrir. Al-Minshawi's Mujawwad style tends to show more of these vs. the more measured Murattal style.
